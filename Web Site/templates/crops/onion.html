Information about Onion

Onion is a widely used vegetable grown for its edible bulbs. It thrives in well-drained sandy loam soil with moderate irrigation. Onions are used in cooking, seasoning, and as a base ingredient in many dishes worldwide. The crop has a strong aroma and medicinal properties.

Best Harvesting Techniques

Onions are harvested when the leaves dry out and fall over. Manual harvesting involves pulling the bulbs from the soil and allowing them to dry before storage. Large-scale farms use mechanical diggers for efficiency. Proper curing is essential to enhance storage life. Onions should be stored in a dry, ventilated area to prevent sprouting and decay.
