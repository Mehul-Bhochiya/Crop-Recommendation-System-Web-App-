Information about Tomato

Tomato is a popular vegetable crop used in various culinary dishes, sauces, and processed products like ketchup. It grows well in warm climates with well-drained soil and moderate watering. Tomatoes are rich in vitamins and antioxidants, making them a nutritious food choice.

Best Harvesting Techniques

Tomatoes are harvested when they reach full color and firmness, depending on the variety. Manual picking is preferred to avoid bruising the fruits. In large-scale farming, mechanical harvesters collect ripe tomatoes efficiently. Harvested tomatoes should be stored in a cool, dry place to extend shelf life. Proper handling reduces post-harvest losses and maintains fruit quality.
