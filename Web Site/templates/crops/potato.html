Information about Potato

Potato is a widely grown tuber crop rich in carbohydrates. It thrives in cool climates with well-drained sandy loam soil. Potatoes are cultivated for various food products, including fries, chips, and mashed potatoes. The crop is propagated using seed potatoes, which sprout into new plants.

Best Harvesting Techniques

Potatoes are harvested when the leaves turn yellow and start drying. Manual harvesting involves digging up tubers using spades, while mechanical harvesters speed up the process in large farms. After harvesting, potatoes should be cured in a cool, dry place to toughen their skin. Proper storage in well-ventilated, dark conditions prevents sprouting and decay.
