The suggested crop is Carrot

Information about Carrot

Carrot is a root vegetable rich in vitamins and antioxidants. It grows best in loose, sandy loam soil with adequate moisture. Carrots are consumed raw, cooked, or processed into juices and snacks. The crop requires proper thinning for uniform root development.

Best Harvesting Techniques

Carrots are harvested when they reach their desired size and color. Manual harvesting involves loosening the soil and pulling the roots carefully. Mechanical harvesters are used for large-scale production. After harvesting, carrots should be washed and stored in cool, humid conditions to maintain freshness. Proper handling prevents bruising and spoilage.
