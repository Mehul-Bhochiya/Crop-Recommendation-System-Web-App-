The suggested crop is Spinach

Information about Spinach

Spinach is a leafy green vegetable known for its high vitamin and mineral content. It thrives in cool temperatures with well-drained, nutrient-rich soil. Spinach grows quickly and can be harvested multiple times in a season. It is used in salads, soups, and cooked dishes.

Best Harvesting Techniques

Spinach is harvested when the leaves reach full size but remain tender. Manual harvesting involves cutting the outer leaves while allowing inner leaves to grow. Mechanical harvesters are used in large farms for bulk production. Freshly harvested spinach should be stored in refrigerated conditions to maintain freshness and prevent wilting.
